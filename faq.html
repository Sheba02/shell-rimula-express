Ordering & Payment

How do I place an order?

To place an order:

  1. Browse our products and add items to your cart
  2. Click on the cart icon and review your items
  3. Proceed to checkout
  4. Enter your delivery details and payment information
  5. Confirm your order

You'll receive an order confirmation email with all the details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Mobile Money (MTN, Airtel)
  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard)
  • Cash on Delivery (within Kampala)
  • Bank Transfer
Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 30 minutes of placing it by contacting our customer service at +256 700 123 456. After this time, your order may already be processed for delivery.

Is there a minimum order amount?

For delivery orders, we have a minimum order amount of UGX 15,000. For in-store pickup, there is no minimum order amount.

Delivery

What are your delivery areas and charges?

Delivery Charges:

  • Free delivery for orders over UGX 50,000 within Kampala
  • UGX 5,000 for orders below UGX 50,000 within Kampala
  • UGX 10,000 for delivery to surrounding areas
  • Contact us for delivery charges outside greater Kampala

Delivery Times: Same-day delivery for orders placed before 2:00 PM

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location:

  • Same-day delivery: For orders placed before 2:00 PM within Kampala
  • Next-day delivery: For orders placed after 2:00 PM or for areas outside central Kampala
  • Express delivery: Available for urgent orders (additional charges apply)
Can I track my delivery?

Yes! Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive an SMS with a tracking link. You can also track your order through your account on our website or by calling our customer service.

What if I'm not available during delivery?

If you're not available, our delivery team will:

  • Attempt to call you to arrange an alternative time
  • Leave the package with a neighbor (with your permission)
  • Return to our store for you to pick up later

Additional delivery attempts may incur extra charges.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of purchase for most items, provided:

  • The product is in its original packaging
  • The product is unused and in resalable condition
  • You have the original receipt or order confirmation

Note: Perishable goods and personal care items cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

How do I return an item?

To return an item:

  1. Contact our customer service to initiate the return process
  2. Package the item securely in its original packaging
  3. Bring it to our store or schedule a pickup (free for defective items)
  4. Once inspected, we'll process your refund or exchange
How long do refunds take?

Refund processing times:

  • Mobile Money/Card refunds: 3-5 business days
  • Cash refunds: Instant when returning to our store
  • Bank transfers: 5-7 business days

Products & Quality

How do you ensure product quality and freshness?

We take quality seriously:

  • Daily deliveries of fresh produce from trusted local farmers
  • Regular quality checks and expiration date monitoring
  • Proper storage facilities with temperature control
  • Direct partnerships with reputable brands and suppliers
Do you offer organic products?

Yes! We have a dedicated section for organic products including:

  • Organic fruits and vegetables
  • Organic dairy products
  • Organic grains and pulses
  • Natural and chemical-free personal care items

Look for the "Organic" label on our products.

Can I request a product that's not listed?

Absolutely! We welcome product suggestions. You can:

  • Use the contact form on our website
  • Call our customer service
  • Speak to our store manager during your visit

We'll do our best to source requested products for you.

Account & Security

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy and free:

  1. Click on "Register" in the top navigation
  2. Fill in your details (name, email, phone number)
  3. Create a secure password
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Start shopping!

With an account, you can track orders, save favorites, and get exclusive deals.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click on "Login" and then "Forgot Password." Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. If you need further assistance, contact our customer service.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take your privacy and security seriously:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure payment processing
  • We never share your personal information with third parties
  • Regular security audits and updates
