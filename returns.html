Return & Refund Policy

Hassle-free returns and refunds for your peace of mind

Quick Policy Summary

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy, we're here to help.

7-Day Return Window

Return most items within 7 days of delivery

Easy Returns

Simple return process in-store or via pickup

Quick Refunds

Refunds processed within 5-7 business days

Free Returns

Free returns for defective or wrong items

1. Return Eligibility

1.1 General Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • In its original condition and packaging
  • Unused and unopened (unless defective)
  • Accompanied by the original receipt or order confirmation
  • Returned within 7 days of delivery

1.2 Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Perishable goods (food, beverages, flowers)
  • Personal care items (for hygiene reasons)
  • Gift cards and vouchers
  • Customized or personalized products
  • Software and digital products
  • Items marked "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"

2. Return Process

Follow these simple steps to return an item:

1

Contact Us

Call our customer service or use the online return form within 7 days of delivery

2

Package Item

Place the item in its original packaging with all tags and accessories

3

Return Item

Bring to our store or schedule a pickup (free for defective items)

4

Receive Refund

Get your refund processed within 5-7 business days

3. Refund Policy

3.1 Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund. The timing depends on your payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • Mobile Money: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 5-7 business days
  • Cash Refunds: Instant when returning to store

3.2 Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued in the following cases:

  • Item is not in original condition
  • Item is missing parts or accessories
  • Item shows signs of use or damage

4. Damaged or Defective Items

4.1 Immediate Reporting

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please:

  • Contact us within 24 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage or defect
  • Do not use the product

4.2 Replacement or Refund

For damaged or defective items, you can choose:

  • Replacement: We'll deliver a new item at no extra cost
  • Refund: Full refund including delivery charges

Free return pickup is available for defective items.

5. Wrong Item Received

If you receive the wrong item:

  • Contact us within 24 hours of delivery
  • We'll arrange free pickup of the wrong item
  • We'll deliver the correct item as soon as possible
  • You'll receive a apology gift for the inconvenience

6. Return Shipping Costs

6.1 Free Return Shipping

We cover return shipping costs for:

  • Defective or damaged items
  • Wrong items delivered
  • Items that don't match description

6.2 Customer-Paid Returns

You are responsible for return shipping costs if:

  • You changed your mind about the product
  • The item no longer fits or meets your needs
  • You found a better price elsewhere

7. Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for:

  • Different size or color (subject to availability)
  • Different product of equal value

Exchanges are subject to product availability. If the desired exchange item is not available, we will issue a refund.

8. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within the expected time:

  • Check your bank account or mobile money again
  • Contact your bank or mobile money provider
  • If still missing, contact our customer service

Need Help With a Return?

Our customer service team is here to assist you with any return-related questions or issues.

Return Support Hours: Monday-Saturday, 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
