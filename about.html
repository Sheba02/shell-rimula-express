About Shell Rimula Express

Your trusted partner for quality products and exceptional service since 2010. We're committed to making your shopping experience convenient, affordable, and enjoyable.

Our Humble Beginnings

Shell Rimula Express started as a small neighborhood store in Kampala with a simple mission: to provide fresh, quality products at affordable prices to our community. What began as a single store has grown into one of Kampala's most trusted supermarkets.

Founded by Mr. Sheba Mugisha, our supermarket has always believed in putting customers first. We understand that every shopping experience matters, and we strive to make each visit better than the last.

Our Growth Journey

Over the years, we've expanded our product range to include everything from daily groceries to electronics, fashion, and household items. We've embraced technology to bring you convenient online shopping while maintaining the personal touch that made us successful.

Today, we serve thousands of satisfied customers across Kampala, offering both in-store shopping and convenient delivery services to your doorstep.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide our customers with the highest quality products at competitive prices, delivered with exceptional service that exceeds expectations. We aim to be the most trusted and convenient shopping destination in Kampala.

Our Vision

To become Uganda's leading retail chain, known for innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. We envision a future where shopping is seamless, enjoyable, and accessible to everyone in our community.

Our Values

Quality First

We never compromise on quality. Every product on our shelves meets our strict standards for freshness and excellence.

Affordable Prices

We believe everyone deserves access to quality products without breaking the bank. Our prices are always competitive.

Customer Focus

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. Your satisfaction is our ultimate measure of success.

Trust & Integrity

We build relationships based on trust, transparency, and honesty in all our business dealings.
